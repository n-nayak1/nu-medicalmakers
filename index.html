Our Mission

We aim to design innovative solutions to real-world healthcare challenges by collaborating across disciplines and partnering with patients and clinicians. From idea to impact, we build devices that make a difference.

We empower students with hands-on experience in medical device design. By prioritizing upskilling through the prototyping process, we offer members the opportunity to build technical skills while turning their ideas into impactful solutions.

Open to all enrolled Northwestern students, regardless of major or experience level.
